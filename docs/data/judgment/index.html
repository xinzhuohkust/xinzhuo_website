Overview

The author has colleted tens of millions of court judgments by province and by law from 2014 to 2023 with web scraping. For more technical details about the web scraper, please refer to my blog.

By comparing the data in the annual work report presented by the Supreme People’s court (最高人民法院) to the National People’s Congress (全国人大) each year with the data made public on the China Judgements Online, I estimate the pulication rate of legal documents in China. Moreover, based on my own database, I also estimate how many previously published legal documents have been deleted by China Judgement Online.
